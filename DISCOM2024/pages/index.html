   ABOUT COMPUTER SOCIETY OF INDIA

Computer Society of India (CSI) is the oldest and largest organisation of IT and allied professionals in India. CSI is a non-profit professional society formed dedicated to Computing to exchange views and information, learn and share ideas.

OUTLINE OF TOPICS COVERED

- Cyber Security from perspective of Cloud
- Blockchain, IoT & Big Data and AI
- Awareness of new threats and how to mitigate them
- Discussion on Cyber Law and its outreach
- Best practices to follow in organizations and personal life
- Walkthrough of real life case studies
- Panel discussion by leaders on latest cyber security issues & policies
