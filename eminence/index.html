About EMINENCE '26

Eminence is hosted by IETE Kolkata Center in technical collaboration with ACM Kolkata Chapter, CSI Kolkata Chapter, ISTE WB Section. It is a half day event spanning highly reputed speakers from Industry, Research and Academia. Past Eminence: Link
