About EMINENCE '24

Eminence is hosted by IETE Kolkata Center in technical collaboration with ACM Kolkata Chapter, CSI Kolkata Chapter, ISTE WB Section. It is a half day event spanning highly reputed speakers from Industry, Research and Academia.

The Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE) is Indiaâ€™s leading recognised professional society devoted to the advancement of Science and Technology of Electronics, Telecommunication & IT, founded in 1953. Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is the world's largest educational and scientific society in computing, uniting computing educators, researchers and professionals to inspire dialogue, share resources and address the field's challenges, founded in 1947. Computer Society of India (CSI) is the first and largest body of computer professionals in India, founded in 1965. The ISTE WB section and ISTE chapters in West Bengal are consistently involved in promoting and enhancing the quality and esteem of technical education.
